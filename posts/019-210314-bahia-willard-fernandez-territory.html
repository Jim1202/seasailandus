210314 BAHIA WILLARD " Fernandez territory"

 The afternoon beach shore break give us a wet experience and we decide to move to the better anchorage of Bahia Willard,  a bit more north. The Fernandez family (18 members) lives permanently here n keep the trailer park n a few gringo light"houses". Incredible: nobody when landing the dinghy, but a few minutes later, in front of what it can be a restaurant: an old women arrive from nowhere with a mask covered face and a bottle of gel in the right hand. "I can make any food but only if you eat outside". We got a tasty meal including some french fries for Pierre. Nice afternoon walk for us except Helena who manage to connect a cheap internet 15mn per 15mn with mini papers to get lat infos about Hong Kong situation..
