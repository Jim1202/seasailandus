lundi 9 février 2015

150208 SANARY FRA "ligue PACA" (420) 228 pict.

150208 lig SANARY FRANCE (228 pict.) 

137km/h mistral in Avignon this sunday...Courageous Sanary club - a famous club nested in such a tiny sunny space - start on time the first race at 11am. 
Wahoo ! I follow the PACA circuit since 2010, but the med never offered to me  such a magnificent show: Huge long waves under a glorious sunshine. But there are collateral damages: My autofocus is out and I understand with my froozen hand how hard it should be for the racers to sail to windward marker as  this wind is bloody heavy and cold !  Worst...even without moving the RIB, some waves manage to break on board...dangerous for the NIKON, anyway I took the risk with various result.


  The regatta was cancelled during the first race due to a mooring accident on board of commity boat; fortunately nothing more than a in/out to hospital for a hand stitching.
 A bit later some 420 volunteers sailed again for a wild training  but not for long: The sea is really dangerous, especially for the RIBS as it's really breaking anywhere. Coaches safely led everybody - even the former world champion - back into the small and cosy marina. Thanks Sanary, we hope to see you next year !
