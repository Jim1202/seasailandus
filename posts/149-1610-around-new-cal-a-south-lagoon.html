1610 AROUND NEW CAL A: SOUTH LAGOON



"TORFOUYOU" means nothing but was the name of our first multihull bought with my brother Franck in the seventies: the Catyak from taburmarine , a very small boat with a kind of Optimist sail...Now Franck own an OUTREMER 45 named again "TORFOUYOU" based all year round in Port du Sud Nouméa ... We could cruised around New Caledonia, first in the lagon sud, a place hard to beat if you like wilderness n sea.  
Our first TORFOUYOU...our first sailing and freedom hours  in baie of Cannes in the late seventies...

40 years later, Franck, my younger brother  shows his new TORFOUYOU in New Caledonia: an OUTREMER 45
