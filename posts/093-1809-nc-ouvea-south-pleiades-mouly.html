1809 NC OUVEA South Pleiades (Mouly)

Not many real anchorages on these south pleiades island controled by Mouly tribe, but the light permanent wheather authorize to anchor the cat almost anywhere; as soon as you leave the Mouly bridge anchorage, no more cruising boats; we just received the visit of a local speed boat on one of the first island (where the mast could be seen from the village) but giving the name of the chief was, as expected  as a sesame.
 

The groupers are caught...trolling in OUVEA lagoon !

Few hours island hopping in the lagoon: This is what we re here for!
After a few days, crew is now perfectly synchronized for anchoring ...
