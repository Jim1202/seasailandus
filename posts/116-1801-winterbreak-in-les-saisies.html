1801 WINTERBREAK IN LES SAISIES

ALCYONE is on a dry dock . After this tough first season, the best way to keep the cruising motivation at highest level is to make a break: A complete break: change season from austral summer to european winter as far as in "Les Saisies"France where our small family lived pemanently 4 years (2004-2008) just after the circum navigation of "PAPOOSE"(1998-2004).
January 2018 was a powder month in LES SAISIES: Snow, friends, ski... and return to good food just under the Mont Blanc !
