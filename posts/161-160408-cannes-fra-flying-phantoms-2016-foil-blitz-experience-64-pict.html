160408 CANNES FRA Flying Phantoms 2016 (Foil) : blitz experience 64 pict.

Cantin and Arthur: Enthousiasm, Talent, Results ...with micro budget !
What a nice story !
2 very young sailors Cantin ROGER and Arthur Boc HO met 1st time  on the RED BULL french selection last year...and won ! This crew ll represent FRANCE in RED BULL world final, 16 october. in Newport USA.
Cantin (1995) is a pure "cata child" and one of the best F18 present racer; Arthur BOC HO (1998) has a top level in acrobatic 29er; both have a real "fun power" wich really deserve a sponsor to help them to face new challenges among many super pros as on this Cannes race.

 I had only this friday afternoon to spend around this 3 days event and very strict rules about spectators and coach boats shortened the time on water.  Restricted rights given to photos display is major reason why I can offer on this blog only pictures of FRA 1 as this crew used my Lomac as a coach/photo boat and gave me authorization.

With this lack of time, all restrictions, and nobody on board to help, not easy to find the "right place to be" for a photographer and this album (sea link on last picture) is just a short try.

Let's hope private sponsoring as this RED BULL world initiative RED BULL foiling generation can continue to help young sailors to enjoy these magic but expensive machines !

Cannes helped staff of YCC, offering sun and good wind conditions on this friday afternoon.
Check YCC Flying Phantoms in Cannes to have much more and the end of the story with results.

Anyway, you have to see some flights with your own eyes...The speed is phenomenal !
