150421 CIMA (420) BANDOL FRA
 The CIMA (coupe internationale de la mediterranee et de l'amitie) is one of the most competitive regatta in France.
  The prize for the top crews is to participate directly to the summer 420 international events; 
-> the world championship in Karatsu (Japan)
 -> the Eurosaf in Brest (France)
-> the junior european championship in Burgas (Bulgaria)


Christian class president in action
   Next to the casino, The very active club organize around Patrick , Denis and Sandy many events and you can meet in Bandol  during the mild winter various sailing teams from all over North Europe : you can sail almost everyday here...if you add the sun and the charms of the city promenade with the numerous restaurants at a walking distance, no surprise !

 As "special crews", Laurie Candela will participate to her first CIMA...with his 470 sailor father Michel.  
Jules Ducelier ll sail with Alexandre Boite,  his former  teammates in IODA world Optimist championship Boca Chica (see pictures below in 2012), and 2014 laser4.7 world champion !
