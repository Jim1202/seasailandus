140626 KIEL GER "Kiel week 2014 onshore" (420) 234 pict.


140626 Kiel week (234 pictures)

 Achtung Achtung !...Kiel 2014, not the best vintage about wind, anyway I was not at sea with my Nikon…and pictures including compact made are just here for memory.
  But where can you find at the same place so many sailors in such pleasant conditions ? No cars - except the 10km/h tracting Audi quattro, cheap food, free evening entertainment and "bon enfant" atmosphere.  If you add the unbeatable accommodation price with a pure camping experience , you understand as, even without the best sailing conditions,  each real sailor want to come back the next year !
