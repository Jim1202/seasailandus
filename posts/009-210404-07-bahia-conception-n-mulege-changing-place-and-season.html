210404-07: BAHIA CONCEPTION N MULEGE: "Changing place and season"


 BAHIA CONCEPCION is one of the most famous cruising place in Baja...this is also with SANTO DOMINGO anchorage the end of our real cruising in Baja this year.  Covid pushed us in 2020 to rush north and to forget to enter in this huge bay with calm waters, numerous anchorages and golden beaches; this week is"la semana santa" a favorite mexican holiday; even if covid change things: some restaurants disappear,  only a few cruisres around and quasi deserted SANTISPAC parking, a few noisy campers, noria of tourist pangas and excited jet skis move the place in a kind of circus. What a contrast with our precedent stops ! The transpeninsular follows a big part of BAHIA CONCEPCION east coast. Even anchored away from these beaches, you can hear night and day the roaring truck brakes... We anchor here to pay a visit to MULEGE and choice to spend our other nights in AMOLARES and SANTO DOMINGO on the isolated west side: a different feeling about this natural wonder. 

Eastern Holiday: playa SANTA INES- CHIVATO: first cruisers at anchor: climate change !
