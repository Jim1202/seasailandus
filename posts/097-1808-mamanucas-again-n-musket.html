dimanche 10 février 2019

1808 MAMANUCAS AGAIN N MUSKET

    Mamanucas are as dry as Yasawas, but even closer to Nadi airport and Port Denarau. Locals live from a long time from tourism: no dream, money is always the first question to visit anything onshore but inter action is not impossible especially if you don't arrive with a group. 
I spent one year of my life in Rio and it is very hard not climb on this attractive rock, even with a guide; what is a bit sad is to bring my guide spearfishing after the climb: Impossible to fish without a micro spear attached to a finger as there is no more prey from a decent size around.
More southeast in MALOLO LALAI,  Musket cove regatta is one of the most famous pacific regatta, but many things change from 2003.
In the past the rallye FIDJI VANUATU finished the event and this was attracting circumnavigators from all countries; now even the week propose a lot of activities, a majority of the fleet is from New Zealand. Anyway you still receive a life membership of the club as before and locals work hard to organize the week, wich is a good way to have fun and dinner with others; we did'nt participate...Thanks to good managment and work of lot of locals, the resort offer a superb standard, even if you don' sail. ...Overbusy with maintenance to prepare ALCYONE for a good project in New Caledonia we even don't participate this year too... 

Attractives rocks...but no way to climb alone, you need a guide....
