160713-23 SAN REMO 420 CLASS WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP

 160715 WORLD 420: DAY 1 WARM ITALIAN WELCOME IN MARINA AREGAI 


You ll find more than 2800  pictures in free picking about this world event. It ll be the first world championship and the last we ll cover. 
 Quality is uneven especially on the grey minefield of the last days as it's was a real challenge to use the Nikon lens on the small Lomac 4,60 and rush reprocessing sometimes could not create miracles;  anyway I uploaded as usual all pictures a parent on the other side of the planet could be happy to find.



Here we are on the big event each selected sailor wait for. A lot of delegation came safely through Nice airport, including on 14 july and can concentrate now on sailing. May be we all should be happy to  have a sport wich is not concentrating big crowd on a single spot...

 We come back again to Italy, so closed to french riviera but also so different. Everything seems sometimes very complicated but find quickly a solution with nice smiles.
