dimanche 15 mars 2020

2003 LORETO TO CORONADO SOUTH: The first internet alert

After a serious fresh food fill, we can leave LORETO for CARMEN island. The east part  offer a safe but not always confortable anchorage in front of the deserted bahia Salinas. This site is now managed by a hunting operator. A few dozen of hunters come during the cool season in small groups of 4 to hunt the big horn sheep. There are some modern bungalows in front of the beach n behind, leaving on a semi permanent base a small maintenance team. Omar is here and give the permission to hike through the ghost town, the salinas n all the east part of the island as the hunting party remain concentrated on the west part.  
This 2 hours easy hike lead to "playa blanca" of EL REFUGIO, which can be for you alone if no panga arrive from LORETO.  but Omar and his friends gave months of work to clean the impressive quantity of  iron n glass trash we find around the village...

   Heading north lead to CORONADO south anchorage, a few miles away from LORETO with clearwater a lot of fishing birds and...an attractive internet possibility, then punta MANGLES, out of the national park, where an ambitious resort project collapsed a long time ago  n nature is back.

   A few tacks later with a light Northwind, and Alcyone reach to SAN JUANICO, a marvelous and wide bay with many possible places to anchor; we start with the south bays; but we loose internet n knowing what happen in France operate like a drug now , we don't feel so good...A Meeting with cruisers next day in the north anchorage confirm our feeling: A few north american cruisers in the anchorage still don't know what is going with the covid-19...
A light West wind  push ALCYONE along the North coat of CARMEN I

The anchorage of bahia Salinas is very large but not as confortable as it looks like...


Omar is the guy to see on shore if you want to hike

We leave CARMEN to stop in the south of CORONADO island...

Life is everywhere...dolphins cruise around, whales are in the background...
