1708 MAINTENANCE IN THE MAMANUCAS

A break before a crew change,  just to try the cat solo in the Mamanucas n to use the dry weather to continue the refit at anchor in Musket cove: New bilge painting in both hulls, floor poyolight renovation, stronger antiskid in the front and closed to the mast for safety...



Typical micro island in the Mamanucas: Intense tourism, strong organisation...n watermaker !



Before n after the bi components paint of the starboard float's bilges

Alan Marshall with the new code O; designed n cut in NZ, stitched in Fiji...the turbo of any cat







Happy young kiwi officials enjoying a training with the fijian customs in famous Musket cove anchorage
