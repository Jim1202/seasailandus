140524-25 "finale de ligue paca voile legere" EMBRUN FRANCE (276 pict.)

A warm welcome from the locals; we could forget that the water level of the lake was very low. However, optimists, lasers, 29ers, catas, and 420 could fight all the week end. Saturday was perfect day in the mountain, with sun and thermic !
