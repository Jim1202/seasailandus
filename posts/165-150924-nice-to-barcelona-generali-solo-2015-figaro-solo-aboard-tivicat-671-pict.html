jeudi 15 octobre 2015

150924 NICE TO BARCELONA "Generali Solo 2015" (Figaro solo) aboard Tivicat 671 pict.


Georges, friend, offshore cruiser and cat's maniac
Georges  call me to help him on board of  , TIVICAT, the press-catamaran of the  Generali Solo 2015 .
A good opportunity to discover another world of racing in the heart of a pro-organisation: Pen Duick with brillant race director Gilles CHIORRI, former participant himself.
  On this race,  young sailors as Marc MALLARET can play with superpros as Gildas MORVAN and  offshore glories as Alain GAUTIER  .   
All these 22 singlesailors fight with same monotype "Figaro Beneteau 2" (10m long) during 3 weeks of competition including:
-> Costal dayraces in front of Sete, Nice and Barcelona.
-> 2 offshore passages : Sete to Nice (354 m) and Nice to Barcelona (420 m).


GENERALI SOLO 2015 "Aboard Tivicat 1" Nice 93 pict. 
Simple wide angle pictures , mostly about "men at work"and guest aboard Tivicat 
in Nice, Barcelona and offshore with Gilles (boss), Olivia (report), Alexis (photo), Jeff (camera) and Georges,   captain of Tivicat for the event  in absence of the owner, Thierry Peponnet , (470 olympic gold medal Seoul 88) .

GENERALI SOLO "Aboard Tivicat 2" Nice=>Barcelona 105 pict.

Between Nice and Barcelona:
A long waiting party without wind to leave french Riviera, 
then glorious surf before heavy rain with swell  in Barcelona.
This is a game for real sailors,  but not only as they have to fight couple of days later on costal races and olympic type course.
