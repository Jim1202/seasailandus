vendredi 1 janvier 2021

200410-> 210219 COVIDBREAK Long french "covid" break between Cote d'Azur et Beaufortain

After so many month cruising, how delicious is it to enjoy on home shore infinite food choice, best bed and long showers. 

France, avoiding the crowded time, remain an unic playground to share nature with friends around mediterranean sea or in the under Mont Blanc. Covid gave us  enough time to swim, paddle, sail, walk, bike and fly...friends sharing these moments will reconize some places of beloved Beaufortain around our nest in Les Saisies.

We expect to fly back to Puerto Penasco MEX in february 2021 to reach Whangarei NZ next november: Remote Tuamutu island ll be the highlight of this last Polynesian cruise, this time with Helena and Pierre from Bruxelles.
