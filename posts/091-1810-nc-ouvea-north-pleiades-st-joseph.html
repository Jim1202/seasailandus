1810 NC OUVEA: North Pleiades (St Joseph)

  Turquoise is the color of Ouvea and the persistent light n sunny wheather alllow ALCYONE to cruise and anchor even windward of the desert islet of North Pleiades, very closed to offshore deep blue; we are in the wild and  spend a lot of time underwater: Spearfishing In the middle of a school of 2-5kg  jackfishes, we attract bigger predators as dogtooth tunas;  Alain try his luck without any other success than inviting sharks to the event; this time they are of unusual size; we finish on board of the dinghy, happy to have enough jack for the meal and to bring back the spear...
