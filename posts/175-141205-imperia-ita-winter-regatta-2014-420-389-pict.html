141205 IMPERIA ITA "Winter regatta 2014" (420) 389 pict.

141205 IMPERIA  ITA (389 pict.)

Imperia...May be the worst and the best of Italy:

  The worst with the yearly unpleasant disparitions: from cheap pins from 420-trolleys every morning to expensive mobiles if you leave your van unlocked during a single minute.
 The best with a respectable float, a real improvement of organization (especially boat storage) and if you wander around during the long december dark hours, the real secret charm of italian hospitality and way of life:
  Bars, old pre-war shops and delicious restaurant where you are received like a family member.
  This year was windy during 2 first days, difficult with technical sailing especially on rainy saturday.  Last day was more sunny but the shifty conditions prevent commity to launch a last race.

  Rain with poor light reduce pictures to part of men 420 fleet,
