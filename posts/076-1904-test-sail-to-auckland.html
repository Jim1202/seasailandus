1904 TEST SAIL TO AUCKLAND


Jordan our british-american crew stop working in Nelson on the south island n we must meet him in Auckland with Jim who start some school holiday:  a last cruise with Helena n a way to come back all to Whangarei to check if everything is ok on board of ALCYONE. From last year, Auckland is no more a entrance/departure port for yachties n we ll have to leave officialy from Marsden Cove, a few milles away from Whangarei.
Last cruise for Helena with 2 island stops near AUCKLAND 
RANGITOTO offer the bucolic side with a few farms only...
Kiwis are expert to create responsable tourism : With such facilities, who can choice to get away from the main path?
WAIHEKE island offer opposite with sophistication, you can find french bread, wine n camembert here...at a price !
This is the season of Feijoa in north island
Welcome again for ALCYONE at the City of Sail with 30k+ gust,,,
...may be too much for this smiling crew...

...but not for this "national floating museum" with one reef only, full of happy tourists, driven by experts.
