mardi 2 mars 2021

210302 LIFE IN DRY DOCK:"2 semanas+ trabajo ! "

 The Boat is in good condition immaculate inside...but very dirty outside, no doubt: PUERTO PENASCO is in the middle of the desert !

  2/3 weeks are normal to prepare quietly the cat; this is the time we need to prepare and a quasi 8 month autonomy without touching any marina.  This time we are 3, we ll carry another outboard, another gas bottle and more food. We ll just make a top up in Puerto Vallarta end of the month before heading to Gambier.

 The shipyard has an incredible potential with cruisers: Super location out of the hurricane belt, not far from USA, quick delivery from Dan Diego, Phoenix or Luckeville, relative cheap mexican prices...Salavdor 3 (third of the CABRALES generation) can move this ex shipyard for shrimpers in a mecca for cruisers.  lot of work, but after only 3 years, there are already 70 boats here...

SHIPYARD: American style cruising: the bicycles, the boat behind the huge camping car



This is the way we found ALCYONE in ASTILLEROS CABRALES: Intact, dry and dusty  


But water is available and free here, and ALCYONE is cleaned from the mast to the deck...









Fortunatly for the crew, good crew compensation exist 2 blocks away from the shipyard !


trying the jib...

Some damages on boltrope...


Inspection needed...light n agil Helena is up with Pierre's weight control 



In fact, the old furler worked a lot from Auckland to Vancouver and aluminium sections of profile turned a bit beginning to deteriorate the jib boltrope; classical, but what is incredible is that we did'nt notice this problem, 10 month ago when covid pushed -derigging  




Solution: Pierre preparing the tent piles, Helena sewing the jib,  and me drilling/riveting the Profil

                                              Then, full mainsail test,  Code O is re-rigged


This time, more Ddlegation : polish to a brother's team ... some epoxy finish to Pancho... 
  

Dry climate offer nice working conditions but impose complete cleaning  after a strong northern

A 2021 new toy with the second generation of e-wincher, so precious as saving 5 electric winches ! 
The handle arrived from France over a week end though efficiency of e-wincher team, very usefull hub of Luckeville USA and service of Salvador junior,  helpfull manager of the shipyard. 



Second 2021 gift: famous Yamaha 15cv 2 stroke enduro, perfectly fitting in the starboard bilge !
Our beloved 4 yars old 9.8hp Tohatsu for the second time offered during servicing clear water instead of oil in the gear box ! How possible as this engine was running each time prefectly ?
the enduro offer more trust, power for a wild Tuamutu project with 2 crews weighting over 90kg.
