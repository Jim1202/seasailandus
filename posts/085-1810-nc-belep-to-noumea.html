1810 NC BELEP TO NOUMEA

  1. Time is a bit running out and we don't stay more than 2 days in Belep before sailing back to Noumea, this time along the leeward coast. The north west part of NC is very attractive with dry wheather, very little human pression and as a result  a lot of marine resources ; we were lucky to find west wind and the pretty long return to finish this circumnavigation of New Caledonia was in fact quick and easy.
