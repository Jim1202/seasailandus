1807 YAGASA CLUSTER

  Not far and inhabited, this system of islets is more simple and a bit more visited by cruisers as no Sevu Sevu required. Entry is wide and 2 main anchorages are possible ; deep lagoon is however very dangerous and a good visibility is needed to enter as very derp in general with a few killer coral patches on the way. Tracking the way in help a lot to leave early a few days later.
  Fishing is good on the way to Yagasa,  but not so good inside: Groups of fijian fishers come for very far and overnight sometimes here.
