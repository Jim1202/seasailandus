mardi 30 mars 2021

210327-30 TIBURON ISLAND n KINO: "Let's sail to the other side"


TIBURON ISLA is already a miss... I'am sure the west coast can offer pretty spots; unfortunatly wind ll blow strongly from the north for the coming hours and we have to point to the south anchorages...

KINO BAY is also to quickly visited: despite an anarchic display, people here are very friendly, n water temperature - tks to shallow sand bottom- is already ok to enjoy.  Above all a discovery of local indian SERI culture (once living in TIBURON island) could justify a specific stay... but in fact Kino bay with good 4G on board was more for us an internet stop between 2 northerns: what's app with family,  blog update...news from our democratic France (thanks mr Pujadas !) and... former democratic Hong Kong,  slowly but mercyless killed by Chinese Communist Party despite all international agrement ...Yes, we are cruising...but still part of this world, our world ! 

What a calm morning in SAN ESTEBAN anchorage!..

...but these tired males made a party all night long,  even activating the radar !



1st reef is on, 30mn later 2reef is needed...
 
...10 miles and 1 hour later ALCYONE is in TIBURON...

...anchorage of La Cruces is now deserted...

...but these wheels traks discourage any walk inside...

...let's move the cat through monument pass...


...to find a better northern protection ...


...in PERROS BAY; this time we ll share spot with these fishers

TIBURON is over...we don't know the island...this was wind choice...


...Next morning: same tack n fast run to KINO BAY...








We know PELICANO island does'nt offer best protection
...
...but no choice with more than 40k gusts...

The 33kg ROCNA + 45m chain in 6m will be ok and we have 4G !

..next day on shore visit does'nt bring much more...

... I came back quick on board after a light food refill 

Mexicans live with the covid, but Tourist are not here...

..leaving these small buisness in a precarious conditions...
