210112 COVIDBREAK VACCINATION: the world race is on... but not everywhere !

12 january: update

  our flights to Mexicali are booked for 19th feb.

 We cannot wait more to join ALCYONE:  let'hope this plan is not delayed as the hurricane season is not to far now;  we have a lot of work tin PUERTO PENASCO, before launching the cat, heading to PUERTO VALLARTA and crossing again Pacific (4th time) to reach the final destination of Whangarei in New Zealand end october. The shipyard ASTILLEROS CABRALES is waiting for us !

We have to wait...

How returning in Puerto Penasco to prepare the cat without vaccin before 9 month of cruising ?

Mexico is still seriously covid infected.  Even if we fly now, if we play with the virus and if we leave this country before next hurricane season to cross pacific ocean, even if we enter in Polynesia after 3 weeks of forced on board isolation,  what to do after after ?

All Western Pacific island as Cook, Samoa, Kiribati, Tuvalu...and our final destination New Zealand where is living our child ll not accept any risk  traveller ...and these islands are right !

Not easy to understand, after the extraordinary performance of this anti-covid-vaccin creation, the slow move of our proud french country regarding this simple question:

How much is the real value of a quick vaccination compared to all daily losses in term of lives, economy and social ?

Any way, who can pretend that mr CASTEX is not a model of "vrai serviteur de l'etat" ?

Everybody know that you want to give us better news !
