210326 SAN ESTEBAN ISLAND: "in search of endemic iguanas"


SAN ESTEBAN in the middle of the sea of Cortes should provide a decent anchorage on the east side and we leave EN MEDIO COVE before day break, a nice downwind sail and we arrive early enough to land - not an easy landing in the middle of stones with a dozen of barking sea lions- and follow a few hours the large arroyo. No choice, Pierre is away and we cannot leave the boat for too long... We should be so happy to do a longer super day trek  in this interesting island, home of endemic iguanas, even if back to the cat, we have to remove many spines from our jogging shoes, some more than 10mm deeply inside the eva soles.

Leaving to SAN ESTEBAN before daybreak..
