210402-03: ISLA SAN MARCOS : "Our Baja Miss"

   We intend to spend time on isla SAN MARCOS,  in front of SANTA ROSALIA. This island seems very attractive for spearfishing despite the gypse mine in the south; unfortunatly the water is nor clear enough. This make the spot very dangerous for us as the charts are still wrong and the guides are not precise enough; usually, we love these conditions, but, here we are not in Polynesia and the water is tricky: Nothing is more dangerous than believing its clear enough, when in fact its hardly 2m visibility and even at midday you see the danger too late. ALCYONE, returning from a second anchorage in the north, at 5 m only from her paws almost loose a rudder on this pinacle quite far from the shore : 27 14' 973N - 112 06'291W;  it was a questions of a few centimeters...this is the second alert after Puerto Refugio, we have no right to play like that with the cat ! Choice is simple: moving the cat to anchor at sight of the dinghy (Helena is alone) or leaving to far with the dinghy...both solutions are not suitable and we ll leave shortly... This island anyway gave us an unic isolation feeling :  we could ear all the night the shuffle of the whales, following very closed to the shore the west coast of the SAN MARCOS island.
