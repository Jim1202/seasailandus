1806 OGEA WILD SIDE

After visit to the village, it's time to enjoy the huge lagoon; we could stay alone during these days n this gave us one of the best feeling we got in a cruise.
  OGEA offer a rare particularity with a permanent wide waterfall inside the lagoon. This give a nice spot to collect shells including the delicious edible greenturbo (polynesian Maoa) never living so far inside the lagoon. 
A rare curiosity in OGEA: The permanent wide waterfall inside the lagoon, giving a very original biotope  





This tide waterfall change but never stop giving opportunities for classic species of the lagoon 


but also unexpected n delicious polynesian names "Maoa"



Local fishers came to see us to sell lobsters to complete the shell harvest


Engine block is often the last part you can see from an old wreck



OGEA offer also nice beaches too 
God save OGEA !
