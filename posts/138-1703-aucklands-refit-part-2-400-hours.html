jeudi 26 juillet 2018

1703 AUCKLAND'S REFIT (part 2) 400 hours

Alcyone ll anchored in front of Bays water marina n after Jim's university start, we continue the refit with almost daily dinghy roundtrip to Auckland technical zone on south shore:

 Changing plexiglass of 2 main hatches, renovating n vernishing inside woodenparts, changing chainlocker hatch, renovating parts of poyolight floor, fixing new port toilet, cleaning and new painting of all port n starboard float bilges, replacing of 4 bilge pumps, change all indoor cabin lights for low consumption leds,  renovating white pvc back hulljoints,fixing 8kg pressurised aluminium gastank for sodatream system with custom fittings (kitchen),  fixing 220 system in 2 cabins, new microwave, ordering new PVC waterproof bimini top with shade sides , organising complete maintenance/sparepart/tool boxes  for 100% autonomy...

 Buying: New 6 personne ultralight WAYPOINT safety raft, 2 new self grip LEWMAR handles, PROFURL furler spare parts, new 14mm 4 legs hoist with LEWMAR blocks (boom safety), 2  fast opening HARKEN blocks for Code O sheets, NAUTILUS waterproof bimini top with shading sides,  shore/offshore fishing gears, snorkling n spearfishing equipment, RED inflatable paddle board, complete  maintenance library  n west pacific guides...watching daily training of future America 's cup winners
