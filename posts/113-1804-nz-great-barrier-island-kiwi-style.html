1804 NZ GREAT BARRIER ISLAND: KIWI STYLE


   Great Barrier island: Any NZ cruiser ll talk about this place, not far offshore of North Island: ALCYONE had to sail there before her trip to FIJI; a nicer place than any marina to arrange everything on board and to enjoy the best of kiwi spirit: One famous anchorage "of the island "smokehouse bay" offer a real heated bathroom to cruiser; you just have to enjoy it and leave it ready for next one. A first lesson of civism and friendship. 
   Next to us famous STEINLAGER drop anchor: This legend monohull could relax in a museum, but she is still sailing a lot;  3 to 4 dozens of teenagers use her to learn a very spartiat way to cruise; sure that this anchorage give to the crew an unic opportiunity to enjoy a hot shower.
This is the second lesson: how New Zealand use the young generation to carry the young history of the country; after all no wonder about these values...Is not Nea Zealand the country of rugby and sailing ?
