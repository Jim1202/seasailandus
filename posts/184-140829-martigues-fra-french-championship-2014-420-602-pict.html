140829 Chpt de France espoir 420 MARTIGUES (602 pictures) 

Easy to understand why this major 420 event arrive in Martigues again in 2014 as the place offer a lot of positive points, including a very helpful team. 

5 places among 6 on both podiums for PACA team give the level of coaching of Bilou and Julien and super 2014 domination of Hippo and Sidoine. Don't forget to leave the club to have a walk in surprising Martigues, the place is also connected to the med by a water channel !
