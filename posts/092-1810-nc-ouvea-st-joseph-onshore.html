1810 NC OUVEA: St Joseph onshore

Impossible for u to visit teh "North Pleïades" without a visit to St Joseph tribe, owner of these islands and also of "Beautemps Beaupré island further north. With such light wind, no problem to sail back east and we overnight in front of the long white beach before making  again "coutume". Mme Underwood is in charge since the death of her husband; a real good time with a gift exchange; fresh veggies and we can find also bread  in local shop with difference prices regarding the freshness of the "baguettes".
