lundi 22 mars 2021

210312 SAN LUIS GONZAGUE": "Feeling of australian outback !"

 South is the only direction for us: with the still dominant north winds, mornings are chilly... not much above 10 degres;  The first move before breakfast is to open the heating...fortunatly 2 hours later the sun relay. Bahia Sam San Luis Gonzague will be our next stop .


        Yellow: our 2020 covid rush northward; Orange our cruise southward to SL GONZAGUE



We have to use the engines a big part, but wind catch up at S L Gonzague latitude


                                         This is wild west coast of North Cortez gulf

                                                  Hydraulic micro fuite simple solution...


       Strange cloud formation when Sailing again westward to Bahia San Luis de Gonzague, 

Next morning after a peacefull night in a north cove ...

What a beach !...

...for a few lucky wooden houses n trailers...

                     



...but sea water temperature in Bahia S Luis de Gonzague is below 15 degrees !


In fact activity come from the new road, 1 mile inside

A road train as we can find in Australian outback !




 "RANCHO GRANDE" inside solid fences, Petrol station the place to park, to buy... 

...to (try to) sell and survive...

...to have lunch...and to connect internet !

at a reasonnable rate, much cheaper that the lonely hotel on the beach, even with a boat view !
