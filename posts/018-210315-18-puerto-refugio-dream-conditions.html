210315-18 PUERTO REFUGIO: "Dream conditions"

 Again we use the engine most of the time to reach Puerto Refugio, even with a start before daybreak, we want to use the very calm wheather forecast on the spot. Camaroneros also start early...Puerto Refugio has only one other boat in the multiple possible anchorage...conditions are improving everyday in temperature and dominant Northwind make a pause...what else ? 

An important detail anyway: don't trust the charts in north Baja; absence of sounding is really destabilzing and, arriving in west anchorage, fortunatly with calm wheather, we were very closed to drop ALCYONE on reef; this rock (29 32'313N 113 33'507W) was 30cm above water at low tide but not visible at high tide...water color appreciation here is not as in Polynesia...the best warning before 9 months of cruising ...warm up is over !
