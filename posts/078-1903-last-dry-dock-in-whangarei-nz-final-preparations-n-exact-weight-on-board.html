vendredi 5 avril 2019

1903 LAST DRY DOCK in WHANGAREI NZ: FINAL PREPARATIONS n EXACT WEIGHT ON BOARD
Working to prepare is an endless job...This time, ALCYONE has almost no more major refit, but w mut prepare it for a huge crossing to Canada; in Whangarei, we can escape NORSAND (definitly the best place to refit if you need a lot of extra works) to prepare with time on a clean concrete bottom and a nice view in SOUTH PACIFIC GATEWAY MARINA. 

South Pacific Gateway Marina is a very nice spot with a inlet view : After 5pm no way to hear other noise than birds (or artificial panic birds noise !)
  

 THE WEIGHT ON BOARD: This is the opportunity to clean, make order n check what we need, may need or never need... a long time I wanted to do it n I do it this year-: weighting ALL what is on board: 

                             This few cents balance could weight almost everything on board 

 The exercise is also very good to balance well the weight and concentrate it as we can in the center of the cat...
Result: We empty before the weight party  100,3kg, including 33 kg of extra ropes, extra Goiot hatch n matress...all gone on "trade me", the very successfull local website n give away.



So many, so much weight (even dry): It's time to check, weight, sale n keep only the right ones...32kg of ropes ll leave ! 


28th march, ready to splash  with 2 people on board , ALCYONE  has a total of 3330 kg this include mooring gears, dinghy, solar panels... but all things (lines) necessary to move the cat with jib n mainsail n 3 extra halyards, a lot of food (375kg):
- port hull: 777kg
- starboard hull: 785kg
- Inside middle (saloon) including 2x220 watertanks : 814
- Outside middle (cockpit) including anchor/chain, dinghy and : 774
- 2 crew: 180
- TOTAL 3330kg (minus excess water only for south latitudes 330 = 3000kg)
- For crossing in the south latitudes,  we ll remove 320 l of water:
-> Neutralizing one of  two watertanks (220l)
-> Using only the second up to 110 l (half) with top up of watermaker
-> keeping untouched the 10 jerrycans of 10l safety means 2650kg, I ll details later what is what in this weight except food and liquid...

 This will reduce the maxi weight to 3010kg , a very reasonable weight regarding the capacity of  cat.

ALCYONE's nuggets: Cans of "confits de canard" from NC

- Why this impressive mostly french "motivational" and dereasonable food reserve of 350 kg? 

If you find very good fresh products in green New Zealand, the can food is mostly imported from Australia:  The crazy percentage of sugar in the australian food especialy canfood pushed me to buy - at a price- in New Caledonia . The disaster of australian food is obvious when you see the young generation with a quantity of fat kids and teenagers and this give the sad feeling that the healthy blond autralian surfer image  is more a myth than a reality now ...
