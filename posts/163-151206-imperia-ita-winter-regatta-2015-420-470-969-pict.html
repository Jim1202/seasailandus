IMPERIA WINTER REGATA 2015: A RECORD



151206 DAY 1 470 (61 pict.)

151206 DAY1 420 L (116 pict.)
Wahou... More than 200 boats (470 n 420) of 10 countries this year in Imperia ! Coming 420 world championship in SAN REMO, a few milles away is a reason : The light wind, and couple of  "grey" days, including rain on monday did'nt stop fierce fight during these 3 days of competition...

Huge starting line as about 100 open 420) offer a challenge not only for crews but for committee !

No spray and poor light conditions give also a lot of homework in  picture processing and quality loss, but these 1000 pictures on line, with almost everybody, can give a good  idea of action.
 Logistic should climb at level of participation: Some strong improvment could be done as on east side dock for sunday evening rush; but nothing wich can prevent an ambitious european team to participate .

Bravo again and arriverderci !

 (relay from Imperia)
