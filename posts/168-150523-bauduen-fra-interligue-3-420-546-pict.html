dimanche 10 mai 2015

150523 BAUDUEN FRA "Interligue 3" (420) 546 pict.



   
    Don't look for the best smiles of this event on this blog as they are on official sites I invite you to visit as soon you can 

 Given DVD photos Raphael PABLO .  

 What a welcome from the Gendarmes !  

    This remind me the club med of the golden years: The beautiful site without cars, nice view, friendliness as the master world,  and what a lot go "GM" forget, a rigorous teamwork behind all of this with an official photographer; com and protocol were not forgotten with a long local TV report and a real price giving ! 

 It's works very well: Teams from Antibes, Maugio, Aix played together with locals; this time  not only on the water... but on shore around a Petanque and long tables !

 Weather conditions joined the party: Perfect level of water on lake of Serre Poncon, no rain and...a wind ! Who could expect 3 races on the first day with 12k ?

Bernard, Remy and official photographer creating "Convivialite" ...Each team ll leave with a picture CD of the event !

Wind was a bit less generous on 2nd day, but the 420 fleet with the single 28er could enjoy 2 races. 

The light conditions suit best to Lara and Marina from Antibes who choiced the best options....on a single windward sail, you could put half of the fleet behind you ! 
Back on shore, a virgin punch and and a nice dinner were waiting to the competitors...Some guest even tried  the chilly water of the lake !

150525 Bauduen IL Day 3A (118 pict.)
Lucky last day as sun decided to stay all day long above the lake !  The super volunteer staff rushed to prepare the generous sandwiches despite a late bread delivery and racers could improve their "Petanque"  skills from early morning. The wind delivery arrived a bit later and offered 3 races more.
Ok... the 2 last races were very complicated with more than a part of luck, but with a total of 8 races and such a welcome, this interligue ll stay long in PACA sailors memories !
On this monday, Quentin Dalmasso/Loic Reinhard (picture above) got their best day and won race 7. 

The young team Jules Devic/Lucas Beckers from Maugio (picture right) play well on "special" conditions and finished 1st on the last race of the event.
150525 Bauduen IL DAY 3B (114 pict.)



The classic rivality between the 2 leading Antibes boys' boats Maxime PEDRON/Eliot MICHAL (55095) and Jim VINCENT/Victor MAS  (55096) turned this time to the advantage of the first one. 

   About final result, the boys got the best on girls as Quentin DALMASSO/Loic REIHARD got 3nd place, followed by  Marina LEFORT/Lara GRANIER (4th), Jessie KAMPMAN/ Anael PONTHIEU (5th) and Paola AMAR/Carly PEDRON (6th).
