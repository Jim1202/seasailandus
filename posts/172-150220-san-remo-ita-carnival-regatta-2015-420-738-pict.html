150220 SAN REMO ITA "Carnival regatta 2015" (420) 738 pict.

150220 Carnival regatta (420) SAN REMO ITALY (738 pict.)

   

   Marina Aregai in Santo Stefano 
offered numerous advantages to 
organize this 3 days regatta: Very 
closed to attractive San Remo, a lot of parking area including a huge underground space, clean bottom, even a famous sailmaker on the spot...


San Remo onshore (FRA mostly)
  Visitors received a good welcome onshore, but also organisation on the water was at the level as a quick view on the result shows how many BFD were offered to the fleet. Next to a majority of italian teams, including half of the top 10 in last national, 9 french boats behind Julien and Seb joined the party;  
Next 2016 420 world event 
attracted also young delegations as from Germany, Israel, Austria, Russia... 

 Meteo offered a panino of 2 attractive racing days with relative light, but reliable wind around a rainy winter saturday.
150222 San Remo before race 4 (58 pict.)
   With 6 races, more than 60 boats on the line, it was a positive sailing week end ! 
  Absent on friday, and after enjoying the cold rainy saturday, I concentrated international fleet pictures on  the sunny sunday...
