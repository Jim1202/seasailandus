1807 VANUA BALEVU " WILD SPOT ON THE REEF"

Let's sail to one of the reefs north of VANUABALEVU: Trade wind stays around 25k making Passage and mooring are a bit bumpy but the feeling to stay alone and  the spearfishing are great. This is to enjoy such places that we are cruising.
Trade wind is fully establihed now n passes are much wilder


Our next stop: not an easy spot to reach, but as far as the water is clear, still possible.

Attractive sandspit with clearwater around...

...Let's drop anchor !

And close the lazzybag



Our private family place for a few days... 


Fishes are plentyfull here and give choice for dinner

This grouper is just for watching: too big n risky (ciguatera),

Parrotfish, not an easy shot in Pacific: delicious in tahitian rawfishsalad


Rabbit fish and lantanier make also great dishes 

if somebody is here to clean n prepare...
