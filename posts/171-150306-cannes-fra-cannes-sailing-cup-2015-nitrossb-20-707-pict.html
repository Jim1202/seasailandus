150306 Cannes sailing cup (Nitros+SB 20) FRA : 4 races of 1st day only (707 pict.)

 This 3 days regatta is the new event 2015 choiced by the YC  Cannes. 12 boats (SB20) joined the endemic NITROs to offer a good fun and nice show.

As an helper of Pierre, I was on board of his RIB, mostly located on the starting line or closed to the downwindmarker; sometimes frustrating with the glare, but we could move a bit especially during the last race to catch some glorious surfs or simple "souvenirs"of bay of CANNES. (7 albums/707 pict.)
