140418 Cip selection (OPTI) CANNES FRANCE (380 pict.)                                                                                                  

What a beautiful event this CIP ! the best optifrenchies under 16  fight to obtain a place in IODA world and european championship. 

  I helped Jean Claude, former active YCC president to obtain this event in Cannes. Objective was to prepare a yearly ambitious international regatta with first session in october 2016. Jim, my son who finished second last year could easy help to promote the event around the best world optisailors and their families...
