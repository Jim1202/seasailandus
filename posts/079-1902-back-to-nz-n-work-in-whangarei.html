1902 BACK TO NZ N WORK IN WHANGAREI

 We find back ALCYONE on dry dock of South Pacific Gateway Marina in february.  This time, the cat must be prepared for longer navigations: We intend to cross Pacific with objective to be in Vancouver this summer, then San Diego end november after mexican hurricane season to sail Baja and the sea of Cortez before heading back in french Polynesia...next drydock is not forecaste before november 2020 !
   Heater should be install for high latitudes south as north; Guy, a well known french cruiser help us with talent n humour; we also anticipate with him any break (engines)  with change of both 2 cutless bearings, n volvo type point, 4 engine mounts back side; the detroyed port skeg, imported from France was rebuilt nicely by the shipyard with a review of both rudder/daggerboard, we do also preventive change of ropes n weight everything on board.
