dimanche 10 février 2019

1808 LAST YASAWAS

   This is the 7th time I visit the Yasawas: The top north offer superb deserted beaches where we could always anchor without any problem even without any "Sevu Sevu"... but not this year: We anchored not far from the village to present our Kava and affer also a fish we caught in the morning in Blighwaters; the fish immediatly disappear in a freezer and ALCYONE was supposed to be booked next morning for a fishing party...Clear objective of this guy is not only to give good time to his family, but to finish to fill the freezer with our cat and carry full freezer by plane to sell all of that in SUVA !
When I spoke about spoiled kids in FULAGA...I was far to imagine such a proposal. but no wonder : that what a multihull did 2 days before our arrival.
  We immediatly left and anchored a bit more south: the membranes of the watermaker must be changed n ALCYONE need to stay a couple of days without sailing.  
  But this was not finished and we had to give kava night time to somebody from the next village even without landing...this is simply the last time I sail through these islands.


Top north of YASAWAS...not far enough from the top touristic area of FIJI...


YASAWAIRARA...freezers n airport nearby...what else ?  
A quiet place to change membranes of our watermaker ?
Help of U-tube tutorial; Fijian internet is one of the best n cheapest !

But even far from shore n without landing, Kava is requested... at anytime

    We could stay a few days there without any demand or visit, but this time seems to be over
Mini squid, but maxi smart
