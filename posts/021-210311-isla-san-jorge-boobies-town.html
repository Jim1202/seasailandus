210311 ISLA SAN JORGE: "Boobies Town"


                                 Isla san Jorge is only 20 miles more south: our first stop


                        As in many places in Baja,  a noisy sea lion colony lives here...

                                But not only... as numerous boobies choice the island to breed




                  When adult, all these growing creatures ll get the green feet... like papa !



                                                                                                    

















                                                Camaronero anchor in the vicinity; this is the full shrimp season now
