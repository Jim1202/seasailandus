1807 VANUA BALEVU OFFSHORE SIDE

   That is the time for some remote experience : The crew want to fish...We have to tack against the trade when leaving SUSUI but it's  the price to pay for a stay in this friendly village...A good watch is needed to sail windward in the lagoon especially as the morning glare don't help; we can reach the american pass, closed to the top north and decide to anchor behind the reef;
here start the fishing party, and this started much before the reef...
