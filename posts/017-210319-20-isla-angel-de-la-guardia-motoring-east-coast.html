210319-20 ISLA ANGEL DE LA GUARDIA: "motoring east coast"

We leave Puerto Refugio with the same unexpected weather; a few days without northern lift the morning temperature from 11 to 18 degres...no need of the small heater in these conditions...First daylight cruising even with engine on are just delicious; we can anchor in the huge bay of Pulpito, Isla Estanque and isla Partida. The lack of wind push us to use the Yanmar almost all the way south but give us opportunity to enjoy isla Angel de la Guardia with exceptional conditions so early in the season...

 

Incredible morning with oil flat water... 
Yellow markers our way up last year, Orange markers our way down this year

Motoring along the rugged west coast of NE coast of Isla Angel de la Guardia...
anchoring in a mirror...
...first spearfishing result


Stop in huge Pulpito bay...if 60 miles away from anywhere...


\

Ila Estanque in the south with same conditions...

we expect  a fast day run to Bay of Los Angeles...
