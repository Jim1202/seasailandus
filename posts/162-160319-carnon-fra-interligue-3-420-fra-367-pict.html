160319 CARNON FRA "Interligue 3" (420) FRA 367 pict.

160319-20 Interligue 420 MAUGIO-CARNON FRA

3 albums 367 pict.


Benedicte at work with Xavier
One of the 3 yearly "Interligues". Winter is still there even in Maugio-Carnon:  it was a very chilly week end on water with poor grey colors, but last of the 6th races offered sunday a bit of sunshine and lovely dramatic contrast between sails and black storm near "La Grande Motte". Nice welcome from the club as everything seems possible in Carnon; it's also so pleasant to listen southwest accent singing in the VHF...but most of the racers will remember about the very strict jury ! 

26 boats 6 races 3 albums coming now: . Go also to Maugio-Carnon Yacht club Site and discover photos of Benedicte on link:    Les photos de Benedicte BECKERS
