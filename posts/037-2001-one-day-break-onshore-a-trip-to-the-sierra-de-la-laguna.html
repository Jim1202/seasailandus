2001 ONE DAY BREAK ONSHORE: a trip to the sierra de la laguna

Waiting for our spare parts in LA PAZ...we can't resist to rent a car to make a day trip to the SIERRA DE LA LAGUNA...this is a 300km roundtrip drive including 50km on a sand road but it's very interesting to see how is the inside of this moutain in the south of BAJA we can see from so long ; Almost of the following pictures come from the Canyon de Zora; but it's necessary to arrive early on the site to get the best for the light conditions and to escape numerous tourists coming from LOS CABOS, no very far away.
