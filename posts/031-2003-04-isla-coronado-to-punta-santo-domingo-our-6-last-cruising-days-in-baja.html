dimanche 5 avril 2020

2003-04 ISLA CORONADO to PUNTA SANTO DOMINGO: Our 6 last cruising days in BAJA...

  On 22th march, ALCYONE is anchored again n SOUTH CORONADO, about 7 miles away from LORETO: A nice place with a special interest (what a shame for a cruiser) : an internet connection:  

 Our original plan is to continue to sail BAJA northward and enjoy best cruising conditions until mid of May.  At this time, ALCYONE should be be lifted for several months on the  dry dock of ASTILLEROS CABRALES in the fishing harbour of PUERTO PENASCO. 
  This fishing harbour is located in the top north of BAJA, out of the hurricane belt. West mexican hurricane season is officially starting on 1st june and, following an absolute rule, we never cruise in the hurricane zone during hurricane season. We also wish to come back home in France as ALCYONE continuously cruise since our departure from New Zealand... in spring 2019 ! 

   Some friends advise us "to stay on the water where we are", as they are already enjoying in FRANCE the phase 3 of the covid-19 fight...why not ?
  Its true that we can live 1 year+ on board of ALCYONE, even without landing in a harbour: We have unlimited solar energy, a reliable watermaker, there are plenty of fishes easy to spear and a lot of food inside the catamaran...but things are not so simple...
  Covid-19 create a worldwar now, we don't feel anymore to be a part of the BAJA nature but from the human world: There is a war and we begin to feel very guilty not to participate !
Anyway, we still have the plan to leave with our 22th may flight, with the dream to escape MEXICO before the virus war and return to France after...a dream !

We continue northward, but the need of information operate like a hard drug for us; we use early night starts n engine to avoid the daily North wind to reach the next internet connection 65 miles in the North, in SANTO DOMINGO, in BAHIA CONCEPCION, with 2 stops before:
- SAN JUANICO again with our last direct cruiser's contact : famous SSB operator Dough,who spend here with his wife Linda on board of"QUE LINDA" a big part of his cruising time,. 
- PUNTA PULPITO , 10 miles more north a perfect Northern protection, my last NIKON safari...
